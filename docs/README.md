# Welcome to Worldforge

Worldforge is a platform where you describe a world in a sentence and AI builds it into a fully playable 2D game — complete with combat, NPCs, quests, building, vendors, wildlife, and progression. Every world also comes with a deep lore layer you can explore, edit, and grow.

## What You Get

When you create a world, Worldforge generates:

- **Characters** — named entities with lore, roles, and relationships
- **Factions** — political groups with alliances and rivalries
- **Locations** — places on an interactive world map
- **Artifacts** — legendary items with history
- **Species** — races and creatures that inhabit your world
- **Events** — historical moments on a timeline divided into eras
- **A playable 2D game** — walk into your world, fight enemies, talk to NPCs, complete quests, and build structures

## Quick Start

1. Go to [worldforge.fun](https://worldforge.fun)
2. Sign in or click **Enter Demo World** to try it without an account
3. Type a world concept (e.g. "a dying world where the last city floats above an ocean of ash")
4. AI generates your world in ~30 seconds
5. Click **Explore** to walk into your world as a playable 2D game
