# Quests & Missions

Worldforge has 8 quests across all zones, each with gold and resource rewards. Quests range from exploration missions to fetch quests to combat challenges.

## Grassland Mission: Clear the Grassland

The main mission in the Grassland zone. A multi-stage quest that tests your combat and exploration skills.

**Objectives:**
1. Visit 5 Points of Interest (POI discovery banners appear as you find each one)
2. Defeat 7 Orc Warriors
3. Open the reward chest

**Reward:** +75 Gold

After completing the grassland mission, return to the hub for a payoff and to unlock further objectives.

---

## Marina's Lost Necklace (Village)

A fetch quest given by Marina in the Seaside Village.

**How to Start:**
Talk to Marina in the village — she'll tell you she's lost her necklace and ask for help.

**Objectives:**
1. Search the old well
2. Search the dock crates
3. Search the garden flowers
4. Return to Marina with the necklace

You need to search 3 specific spots in the village. The necklace spawns at one of them. Once you find it, return to Marina.

**Reward:** +30 Gold

After completing the quest, a reward chest also becomes available: **+25 Gold** bonus.

**Total Reward:** 55 Gold (30 from Marina + 25 from chest)

## Building Quests (Hub)

Building residents in the hub offer their own quests tied to your progress:

### Helena's Firewood
- **Objective:** Gather 5 wood for Helena
- **Reward:** +15 Gold

### Other Building Quests
As you place more buildings in the hub and attract residents, additional quests become available:
- Survey discoveries for Marcus
- Clear orcs for Barton (the Guard Captain)

These quests are **progression-aware** — they only appear once you've placed enough buildings and attracted the corresponding NPCs.

---

## Quest Tracking

Active quests appear in the HUD as a quest panel showing:
- Quest name and source
- Checkbox list of objectives (checked off as you complete them)
- Reward amount in gold

Quest progress persists across sessions.
