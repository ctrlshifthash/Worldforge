# Vendors & Economy

Worldforge has a full economy with gold, materials, and vendor shops in every zone. Kill enemies, complete quests, and find loot to earn gold — then spend it on gear, potions, and supplies.

## Currency

| Currency | How to Earn |
|----------|-------------|
| **Gold** | Kill enemies, complete quests, open chests, find loot drops |
| **Wood** | Start with 30, earn through quests |
| **Stone** | Start with 15, earn through quests |

Gold is your primary currency for buying items from vendors. Wood and Stone are used for building.

## Vendor Shops

### Hub Merchant
Your main shop. Talk to the merchant and choose the **Shop** option to browse items. The merchant also offers:
- **News** — context-aware updates about the world based on your progress
- **Info** — tips about what to do next

### Grassland Vendor
Located at the Vendor Camp POI in the Grassland zone. Specializes in combat supplies:

| Item | Price | Effect |
|------|-------|--------|
| Health Potion | 15g | Restores HP |
| Strength Tonic | 25g | Temporary damage boost |
| Iron Shield | 40g | Reduces damage taken |

### Fiona's Food Shop (Village)
Located in the Seaside Village. Sells food items.

### Gerald's General Store (Village)
Located in the Seaside Village. Sells a variety of general supplies.

### Witch Willow's Potion Shop (Village)
Located at position (5, 22) in the village. Talk to Willow and choose **"Show me your potions"**:

| Potion | Price | Effect |
|--------|-------|--------|
| Barrier Hex | 30g | Defensive magical protection |
| Third Eye | 20g | Enhanced detection |
| Vigor Brew | 25g | Stamina restoration |

## Earning Gold

| Source | Amount |
|--------|--------|
| Orc kills | Variable per kill |
| Bandit kills | Variable per kill |
| Clear the Grassland quest | +75g |
| Marina's Lost Necklace | +30g |
| Village reward chest | +25g |
| Helena's Firewood | +15g |
| Loot pickups | Variable |

All loot pickups display floating gold numbers showing exactly how much you earned.

## Death Penalty

When you die, you lose a portion of your gold. A "You have fallen... -X Gold" banner displays showing the exact amount lost. You respawn at the hub with your remaining gold intact.

## Tips

- **Stock up on Health Potions** before entering the Grassland — combat is unforgiving without healing
- **Complete quests** for the most reliable gold income
- **Witch Willow's potions** are expensive but powerful for tough fights
- **Gold persists** across sessions, so there's no rush to spend it all at once
